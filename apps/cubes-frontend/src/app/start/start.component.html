Recursive Inscription Cubes

This is a digital art experiment on the Bitcoin blockchain.


Through ordinals and recursive inscriptions, entirely new forms of expression will emerge, and this is one of them. Mint your inscription by combining other images from the chain and enjoy this unique 3D experiment. There is no copyright, no collection, no provenance, no roadmap. Only immutable art.

Mint a cube

Enter six Inscription IDs in the TXIDiN format (eg. 2dbdf9ebbec6be793fd16ae9b797c7cf968ab2427166aaf390b90b71778266abi0) and your receive address (preferably a taproot address which starts with bc1p). We will display an invoice that you can pay directly via 🟧 Bitcoin or ⚡️ Lightning (faster).
After that, we will automatically inscribe your cube and send it to you. It's that simple.
