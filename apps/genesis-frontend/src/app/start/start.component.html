In the beginning

At the turn of the millennium, the artist Olaf Hoppe initiated an epic collection of woodcuts. He interpreted the human existence and the genesis story. Is there a God? Which elements were created first? Are they still around today? Do these elements represent the fundamental truths of our universe?
Each element from Genesis has been put together from multiple prints using the woodcut method.
Explore with us the creation of the woodcuts and continue the story of Genesis.
