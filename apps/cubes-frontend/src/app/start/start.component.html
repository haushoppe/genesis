Ordinal Cubes

The world's smallest onchain gallery, visualized as a 3D cube. Permissionless. Immutable. Forever existing.
Choose six images that are already on the 🟧 Bitcoin blockchain and arrange them into your own cube!

Mint a cube

Enter six Inscription Numbers (eg. 12346327) or Inscription IDs. The six inscriptions must contain an image.
You will find great images on Magic Eden or Ord.io.

After submitting your cube via the "Mint your cube!" button, we will display an invoice that you can pay directly either with ⚡️ Lightning (instant) or onchain with 🟧 Bitcoin (BTC). As soon as the payment is confirmed, we will automatically inscribe your cube on the Bitcoin blockchain and send it to you. The data for the cube is then fully stored onchain and will remain unchangeable forever.
