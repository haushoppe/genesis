Ordinal Cubes

This is a digital art experiment on the Bitcoin blockchain based on the ordinal theory.


Through ordinals and recursive inscriptions, entirely new forms of expression will emerge, and this is one of them. Mint your inscription by combining other images from the chain and enjoy this unique 3D experiment. There is no copyright, no collection, no provenance, no roadmap. Only immutable art.

Ordinals is a project in the Bitcoin world, which has gained a lot of excitement in the last few weeks. Within a short time span, this innovation has once again caught the public's attention to Bitcoin. It is time to examine what ordinals really are and what functionality they bring to the Bitcoin space. For more insights, we encourage you to explore our FAQ section.

Mint a cube

Enter six Inscription Numbers (eg. 12346327) or Inscription IDs in the TXIDiN format (eg. 571fcea04e78d4fb484d46d7efbd00d0baaf418fa6da06ea863eee474a572b83i0) and your receive address (preferably a Taproot address which starts with bc1p). The six inscriptions must contain an image. If a side of the cube turns black, then the image format is not supported. You will find great images on ord.io and on hiro.so.

After submitting your cube via the "Mint your cube!" button, we will display an invoice that you can pay directly either with ⚡️ Lightning (instant) or on-chain with 🟧 Bitcoin (BTC). As soon as the payment is confirmed, we will automatically inscribe your cube on the Bitcoin blockchain and send it to you. The data for the cube is then fully stored on-chain and will remain unchangeable forever.
