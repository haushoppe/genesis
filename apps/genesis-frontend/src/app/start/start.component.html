In the beginning

In the beginning, there was nothing but a formless void, shrouded in darkness and silence. But then, out of this emptiness, a voice spoke. It was the voice of God, the creator of all things. And with each word, a new element was materialized.

First, God spoke the word "light", and instantly, a brilliant burst of energy filled the void, illuminating the darkness.
Then, God spoke the word "water", and great oceans and rivers sprang forth, glistening in the light.
Next, God spoke the word "earth", and the ground took shape, with mountains, valleys, and plains.

As God continued to speak, more and more elements were created… Olaf Hoppe, a renowned artist with a passion for the spiritual and mystical, has created an epic collection of woodcuts that reinterpret the timeless story of Genesis. Using traditional woodcut techniques, the artist crafted an impressive collection of physical artworks. Under the engineering of his son Johannes Hoppe, all works in the Genesis collection and rare off-prints are now available as digital collectables.
